OUR STORY

KA Boutique was started by kavyasri and avanthika !! They are basically from mumbai, did their Fashion Designing from NIFT-TEA. The name KA is formed from the First 2 letters of (K)avya (A)vanthika and This Boutique was founded with the primary objective of designing and manufacturing Ethnic Indian wear and life style products from April 1st 2013. We have more than 2 lakh happy customer base out of which 60% to 80% are regular buyers. Apart from the indian customers, there are regular from across the world. Our boutique offers a wide array of an impressive collection personally curated by Ms kavyasri and Ms Avanthika – who has grown the boutique with tender loving care from a small outlet in 2007 to 750 Sq. feet two storey store in 3 years.











WHERE OUR STORY LED TO
